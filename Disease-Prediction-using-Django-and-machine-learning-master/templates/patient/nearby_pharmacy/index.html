How it works

Green markers on the map show pharmacies that have your medicine in stock. Grey markers are other nearby pharmacies. Click any marker for details. Results stay on this page — no redirect to Google Maps.

Good to know

  • Confirm dosage and interactions with a pharmacist.
  • Use the AI assistant for a plain-language explanation.
  • 24-hour pharmacies are marked with a blue badge.
